    You can easily change how the website looks by switching to dark or light theme

    Regular Holy Mass

    Normally, we hold Holy Mass celebrations on various days, and should you ever wish for us to sing at one of your special occasions, please don't hesitate to reach out.

    Sacrements Holy Mass

    A sacrament is a religious ceremony or ritual regarded as imparting divine grace. The Church recognizes the existence of Seven Sacraments instituted by the Lord: Baptism, Confirmation, Eucharist, Penance, Anointing of the Sick, Marriage, and Holy Orders.
    We receive these Sacraments to renew and strengthen our spiritual lives, bringing us closer to the Lord. These ceremonies are sacred and require a peaceful soul and environment. By celebrating a Sacred Holy Mass, the Holy Father grants us His new gifts to use and transform the world for the better.
    It is our pleasure at ESG to accompany and sing for the Christian community present at these ceremonies during the Holy Mass celebration.

    Wedding Holy Mass

    There are countless unforgettable moments that people experience in life. Among the most significant is the decision to marry - the wedding! This occasion should be incredibly special for everyone involved, particularly for the bride and groom. To ensure your wishes come true, consider inviting us. With our presence, your wedding Holy Mass will become a prayerful memory for a lifetime, cherished not only by you but also by your family. Contact us

    Religious Concerts

    Religious concerts bring peace to our souls, especially during holidays like Christmas and Easter, celebrating the birth and resurrection of Jesus Christ. These occasions remind us of God's love for His people. At ESG, we believe in praising the Lord at any time, not just on these holidays. We enjoy sharing the joy of praising Him through music!
    By expanding beyond holidays, religious groups can foster a sense of unity, creativity, and continuous worship throughout the year, which additionally can serve as a platform to engage with a wider audience and promote interfaith dialogue, spreading the message of faith and community to a broader audience. Embracing religious concerts throughout the year allows for a more vibrant and inclusive religious experience.

    Requiem Mass

    Unfortunately, we may lose our beloved ones when we need them most. In such moments, we should not miss the opportunity to lay their souls to rest in peace.
    Praying for our departed family and friends is an act of love, remembrance, and spiritual connection. It acknowledges the enduring bond we share with them even after their passing and offers comfort during times of grief. It is a practice that allows us to honor their memory, seek forgiveness, and ultimately, as believers, entrust their souls to a higher power. Praying for the deceased is a meaningful way to continue our relationship with them in the spiritual realm and find closure in the midst of loss.
    You can be with us for help with your confort through cereblating a Requiem Mass.
